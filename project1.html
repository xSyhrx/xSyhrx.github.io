Astar Algorithm

At the heart of my project is the A* pathfinding algorithm. A* is a renowned algorithm used to find the shortest path between two points in a grid or graph. In my implementation, I not only utilized A* but also optimized and tailored it to the specific requirements of my simulation. This efficient pathfinding algorithm played a crucial role in guiding AI agents through complex environments.

Steering Behaviours

To make the AI agents' movements realistic and dynamic, I incorporated steering behaviors. These behaviors, including seek, flee, and wander, enabled the AI agents to interact with their surroundings convincingly. By integrating these behaviors into my project, I added an extra layer of sophistication and realism.

Finite State Machine

Managing the AI agents' decision-making processes was achieved through a well-structured finite state machine. The FSM architecture allowed me to define distinct states and seamlessly transition between them based on various criteria. This not only enhanced the control and adaptability of the AI agents but also made the project's codebase more modular and maintainable.
