Ghostography

Ghostography is a spooky atmospheric puzzle game. Play as a photographer that has won a trip to a haunted mansion and been gifted an old analog camera. Capture pictures of ghosts with your camera and complete tasks to escape the mansion.

Ghost Camera Mechanic

At the heart of my project is the innovative Ghost Camera mechanic. Using this mechanic, players can capture hidden entities visible only through the lens of an old analog camera. The camera reveals ghosts, hidden messages, and clues critical to solving puzzles and progressing through the game.
